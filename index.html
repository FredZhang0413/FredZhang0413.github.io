About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Columbia University, advised by Prof. Xiaodong Wang. My research focuses on deep-learning for communication, deep-learning for optimization, integrated sensing and communication, and communication security. I also work closely with Prof. Suman Jana and Prof. Joe Devietti.

I received my B.Eng. and M.Sc. in Information and Communication at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). Before joining Columbia, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Prateek Saxena and Prof. Trevor E. Carlson at National University of Singapore (NUS) and Prof. Xianghang Mi at USTC.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning systems
  • Secure code generation
  • Systems security
